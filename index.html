Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our procedures, insurance, and consultations.

We accept most major insurance plans, including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, and UnitedHealthcare, for medical dermatology and skin cancer treatments. Cosmetic procedures are typically out-of-pocket.

A screening is a quick, visual inspection of your skin from head to toe. Dr. Gladstone or our team will check for any suspicious moles or lesions. It is completely painless and usually takes about 10-15 minutes.

Recovery time varies depending on the size and location of the surgical site. Most patients return to light activities the next day, with complete healing of the surface taking 1 to 2 weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure optimal healing.

Results vary by patient and the specific product used. Generally, Botox results last between 3 to 4 months, while dermal fillers can last anywhere from 6 to 18 months. We will discuss a personalized maintenance timeline during your consultation.

You do not need a referral to book a cosmetic consultation. However, for medical dermatology or skin cancer treatments, your specific HMO or PPO insurance plan may require a referral from your primary care physician. Please check with your insurance provider prior to your visit.

We kindly ask for at least 48 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee, as that time was reserved specifically for you.
